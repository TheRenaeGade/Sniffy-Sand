Sniffy Sand datapack by RenaeGade!

Makes sand, red sand and dead bushes renewable through Sniffers.
When a Sniffer is on sand, they can dig up sand, dead bushes and sticks.
When a Sniffer is on red sand, they can dig up red sand, dead bushes and sticks.
Doesn't change the behaviour for when Sniffers are on moss/grass/dirt/etc..
